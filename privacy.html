Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how SereneAI LTD ("we", "us") collects and uses your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

Data Controller

SereneAI LTD is the data controller. You can reach us at info@sereneai.co.uk.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form we collect your name, email and any other details you choose to provide.

How We Use Your Data

Your details are used only to respond to your enquiry or provide the services you request.

Legal Basis

We process your data with your consent or where it is in our legitimate interest to respond to business enquiries.

Retention

Contact form submissions are kept for up to 12 months and then securely deleted unless we have an ongoing relationship.

Processors

Our site is hosted by Netlify and form submissions are processed by them. We also load fonts from Google Fonts, which may transfer your IP address outside the UK/EU.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Cookies

We use a single consent cookie (uk_gdpr_consent) to remember whether you accepted or rejected cookies. You can withdraw consent by clearing your cookies.
